·DEFINITION ·



Urban transformation is an improvement and renovation of urban

environmentwhich become old and shabby over time.

Those changes being made base on their socio-economic and the physical conditions.

Urban Transformation involves changes that a built environment must undergo,

to meet the growing demands and dynamic aspects that affect it. These aspects,

depending on geographical location of the city, include population growth,

climate change and demographics.

Urban Transformation also serves as a means for recognizing and celebrating

the ethnic and cultural diversities that form a city.
